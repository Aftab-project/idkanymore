About This Treatment

Our Fractional RF + Microneedling Face & Neck treatment delivers powerful skin tightening, wrinkle smoothing, and texture renewal by combining radiofrequency energy with precision microneedling. This advanced dual-action treatment penetrates deep into the skin to firm sagging tissue, remodel texture, and visibly lift the face and neck — all without surgery.

Microneedling creates controlled micro-channels while Fractional RF delivers heat into the dermis, maximising skin tightening, regeneration, and long-term rejuvenation.

Your Step-by-Step Experience

A 6-step skin rejuvenation journey

1

Skin Prep & Activation

The skin is cleansed and prepped to ensure optimal penetration of radiofrequency energy and microneedling treatment.

2

Precision Skin Assessment

Facial and neck areas are carefully assessed to identify laxity, texture concerns, and areas requiring targeted tightening.

3

Fractional RF + Microneedling Treatment

Advanced microneedling creates controlled micro-channels while Fractional RF delivers heat into deeper skin layers, triggering tightening and regeneration.

4

Sculpting & Lifting Focus

Treatment concentrates on enhancing jawline definition, cheek contour, neck firmness, and wrinkle smoothing for visible lifting results.

5

Active Treatment Infusion

Targeted serums are infused into the skin to support repair, improve hydration, and enhance treatment effectiveness while the skin is most receptive.

6

Barrier Protection & Finish

A calming skin-healing layer and SPF are applied to protect the skin barrier, reduce sensitivity, and support optimal recovery.
