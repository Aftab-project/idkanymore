About This Treatment

HIFU Jawline combines HIFU, MPT, MFU, and EMS to lift and sculpt the lower face, reduce jowls, and define the jawline without surgery.

Layered energy tightens skin, tones muscles, and boosts collagen to sharpen contours with minimal downtime.

Ideal for clients seeking a chiseled jawline, cleaner profile, and long-lasting firmness.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about HIFU Jawline Definition

Is the treatment painful?

Mild heat or tingling can occur, and settings are adjusted to keep you comfortable.

How long does the treatment take?

Allow around 4560 minutes including consultation, mapping, treatment, and aftercare.

When will I see results?

You may notice an immediate lift; collagen remodelling sharpens the jawline over 612 weeks.

Is there any downtime?

Minimal downtime. Normal activities are fine; avoid intense heat or vigorous exercise for 24 hours.

How long do the results last?

With 23 sessions spaced 68 weeks apart, results last months; maintain every 46 months.
