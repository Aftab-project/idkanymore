About This Treatment

The Hydrofacial is an advanced, machine-assisted treatment that deeply cleanses, exfoliates, and hydrates the skin while infusing targeted serums for optimal results. Using innovative vortex technology and gentle suction, impurities are removed as the skin is nourished with antioxidants, peptides, and hyaluronic acid.

Suitable for all skin types, this treatment improves skin texture, reduces congestion, and restores hydration for a smoother, more radiant complexion. LED therapy is incorporated to calm redness, soften fine lines, and revitalise tired skin, leaving the face refreshed, plump, and glowing with no downtime.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Skin Glow: HydroFacial

Is the treatment suitable for all skin types?

Yes, HydroFacial is suitable for all skin types including sensitive skin. The treatment is fully customizable to address your specific concerns.

How long does the treatment take?

The complete treatment takes approximately 60-75 minutes including consultation and post-care.

When will I see results?

You'll notice immediate results with radiant, hydrated, and glowing skin right after your first session. Results continue to improve with regular treatments.

Is there any downtime?

No downtime. You can resume normal activities immediately and even apply makeup right after treatment if desired.

How often should I have this treatment?

For optimal results, we recommend treatments every 4–6 weeks for maintenance. Your therapist will create a personalized schedule based on your skin goals.

Recommended Frequency

Maintenance: Every 4–6 weeks for optimal results and sustained radiance.

Combination: Can be combined with treatments like peels, masks, or serums for enhanced effects.
