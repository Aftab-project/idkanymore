About This Treatment

Our professional waxing treatments deliver smooth, long-lasting hair removal using high-quality waxes suitable for even sensitive skin. Each treatment is performed with precision to minimise discomfort, reduce irritation, and leave the skin feeling soft, calm, and impeccably groomed.

Waxing removes hair from the root, resulting in slower regrowth, finer hair over time, and smoother skin compared to shaving. Suitable for face and body areas, waxing is ideal for maintaining a polished, confident appearance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about professional waxing

Is waxing painful?

Most clients experience only mild discomfort that subsides quickly. Our high-quality waxes and professional technique minimise pain, and the area becomes less sensitive with regular treatments.

How long does hair stay smooth after waxing?

Results typically last 3–5 weeks, depending on your individual hair growth cycle and the area treated. Regular waxing can extend this period and result in finer regrowth.

Can I wax if I have sensitive skin?

Yes! We use high-quality waxes suitable for sensitive skin. Please inform us of any skin conditions, and we can adjust our approach. Post-wax soothing products help minimise irritation.

How do I prevent ingrown hairs?

Proper aftercare is key. We recommend our Pre-Wax Enzyme Exfoliation and post-wax products to minimise ingrowns. Avoid tight clothing and maintain regular exfoliation between appointments.

Is there any downtime after waxing?

No downtime required. You can return to normal activities immediately. Just avoid hot baths, saunas, and intense exercise for 24 hours to allow the skin to settle.
