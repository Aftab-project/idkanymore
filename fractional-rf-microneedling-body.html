About This Treatment

Achieve a lifted, sculpted, and firm silhouette with our HIFU Butt Lift treatment, designed to tighten, contour, and enhance volume. Using the latest combination of four advanced technologies — HIFU, MPT, MFU, and EMS — in one treatment, these sessions deliver one of the best non-surgical lifting and firming results available.

This treatment stimulates collagen, remodels tissue, and restores firmness, creating a more lifted, toned, and youthful appearance for the buttocks. Ideal for clients seeking natural contouring, anti-sagging, and enhanced shape without surgery.

Your Step-by-Step Experience

A 6-step treatment journey

1

Skin Prep & Activation

The treatment area is cleansed and prepped for optimal penetration of RF and microneedling energy.

2

Precision Mapping

Sagging, texture concerns, and stretch marks are assessed to customise treatment zones.

3

Fractional RF + Microneedling Delivery

Microneedles create micro-channels while RF energy penetrates deeper layers, stimulating collagen, firming tissue, and smoothing skin simultaneously.

4

Targeted Body Contouring & Lifting

Energy is focused on enhancing definition, lifting sagging areas, and smoothing texture.

5

Active Serum Infusion

Hydrating and healing serums are applied to boost results, support tissue repair, and enhance elasticity.

6

Barrier Protection & Finish

A protective layer and SPF are applied to lock in results, support recovery, and maintain skin health.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about HIFU Butt Lift

Is the treatment painful?

Mild discomfort may occur; numbing cream is available to enhance comfort.

How long does the treatment take?

Plan for 45–60 minutes depending on the treatment area.

When will I see results?

You may notice improved texture immediately; full results develop over 4–12 weeks.

Is there any downtime?

Minimal downtime with possible mild redness for 24–48 hours. Normal activities can resume quickly.

How long do the results last?

Results improve over time; maintenance every 4–6 months recommended.
