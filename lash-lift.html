About This Treatment

Our Lash Lift treatment enhances your natural lashes by lifting and curling them from root to tip, creating the appearance of longer, fuller lashes without the need for extensions. This professional, non-invasive treatment is perfect for anyone wanting a polished, wide-eyed look that lasts.

For clients who want extra definition, a lash tint can be added to deepen lash colour for a mascara-like effect, completing the look for a dramatic, effortless finish.

Your Step-by-Step Experience

A 5-step lash lift journey to beautiful eyes

1

Consultation & Lash Assessment

Your lashes are assessed for length, curl, and natural thickness to ensure the best lift technique and shield size is chosen.

2

Lash Cleanse & Prep

Lashes are gently cleansed to remove makeup, oils, and residue for optimal lift results.

3

Lash Lift Application

A silicone shield is applied to the eyelid, and your lashes are lifted and set with professional lifting solutions, curling the lashes from root to tip.

4

Optional Tint Application

If selected, lash tint is applied to darken and define the lashes for a full, mascara-like finish.

5

Conditioning & Finish

A nourishing solution is applied to condition lashes and protect their natural strength, leaving them lifted, defined, and silky.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Lash Lift

Is Lash Lift safe for natural lashes?

Yes, when performed by a professional, Lash Lift is completely safe and gentle on natural lashes. The treatment enhances what you already have without damage or harm.

How long does the treatment take?

The full Lash Lift treatment takes approximately 60 minutes including consultation, preparation, application, and finishing touches.

How long do results last?

Results typically last 6–8 weeks, depending on your natural lash growth cycle. As your natural lashes grow out, the lifted effect gradually diminishes.

Can I wear mascara after Lash Lift?

Yes! Your lifted lashes look beautiful on their own, but you can wear mascara if desired. The lift makes mascara application even more dramatic.

Is there any downtime or restrictions?

No downtime required. For the first 24 hours, avoid water on your lashes and extreme heat (saunas, steam). Otherwise, you can resume normal activities immediately.
