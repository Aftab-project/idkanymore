About This Treatment

Transform your eyes with our professional eyelash extensions, designed to add length, volume, and curl to your natural lashes. Each set is tailored to your eye shape, lash health, and desired style, giving a natural or dramatic finish.

Extensions are applied individually using professional adhesive for long-lasting results without damage to natural lashes. Whether you prefer the elegance of classic lashes, the dimension of hybrid sets, or the drama of Russian volume, we customise every appointment to your vision.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about lash extensions

Are lash extensions safe for natural lashes?

Yes, when applied and maintained properly by a professional, lash extensions are completely safe. Our professional technique preserves natural lash health.

How long does the initial application take?

Initial full set application typically takes 90–120 minutes depending on your natural lash density and desired style. Infills usually take 45–60 minutes.

How long do lash extensions last?

Lash extensions typically last 3–6 weeks before needing infills, depending on your natural lash growth cycle and how well you care for them.

Can I wear mascara with lash extensions?

Lash extensions look stunning on their own and typically don't require mascara. If you choose to wear mascara, use only water-based products and avoid the lash line.

What aftercare is needed?

For the first 24 hours, avoid water, steam, and intense heat. Afterwards, keep lashes clean, avoid oil-based products, and brush them gently daily with a lash comb.
